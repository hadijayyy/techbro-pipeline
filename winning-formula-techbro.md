# 🏆 Winning Formula — TechBro (@1percent.better)

> Adapted from @parkthebus.football 1.8M-view analysis
> For Indonesian AI/tech audience (20-35岁)

---

## Hook Pattern (Slide 1)

```
[Entity/Perusahaan] baru aja [action konkret].

Tapi [twist/ironi yang gak terduga].

[Angka/fakta spesifik yang bikin geleng].
```

## Contoh yang berhasil:

| Topik | Hook |
|-------|------|
| AI replacing jobs | "OpenAI baru aja rilis model yang bisa gantiin 80% kerja analyst. Tapi yang bikin geleng? Mereka bilang ini cuma 'awal'." |
| Startup burnout | "Startup unicorn ini baru aja PHK 200 orang. Tapi CEO-nya bilang 'ini keputusan terberat'. Gaji CEO naik 3x tahun lalu." |
| ChatGPT productivity | "Google baru aja internal study: engineer pakai AI nulis kode 55% lebih cepat. Tapi error rate naik 40% juga." |

## Kenapa Works:

| Element | Fungsi | Contoh |
|---------|--------|--------|
| `baru aja` | **Immediacy** — sesuatu baru terjadi | "baru aja rilis", "baru aja PHK" |
| `[action konkret]` | **Kredibilitas** — spesifik & konkret | "rilis model", "PHK 200 orang" |
| `Tapi [twist]` | **Curiosity gap** — paksa baca terus | "tapi CEO-nya bilang...", "tapi error rate naik" |
| `[Angka spesifik]` | **Shareability** — bikin orang screenshot | "80%", "55% lebih cepat", "error rate naik 40%" |

## ❌ Anti-Patterns (Kills Engagement):

| Generic (❌) | Kenapa gagal |
|---|---|
| "AI semakin canggih" | Generic, gak ada urgensi |
| "Startup ini menarik" | Vague, gak ada action |
| "Teknologi terus berkembang" | Boring context preamble |
| "Di era digital ini" | Template AI, gak relatable |
| "Bayangin lo bisa..." | Banned pattern |

## ✅ Rewrite Rules:

| Generic (❌) | Winning (✅) |
|---|---|
| ChatGPT semakin populer di kalangan developer | ChatGPT baru aja dipakai 80% developer Silicon Valley. Tapi 40% bilang mereka gak bisa mikir tanpa AI lagi. |
| Startup ini dapat funding besar | Startup ini baru aja dapat $50M. Tapi product-nya belum launch. Duitnya abis buat bayar gaji 200 orang yang kerja 6 bulan. |
| AI bisa gantiin pekerjaan manusia | Google baru aja bikin AI yang bisa handle 90% customer service. Tapi customer complain: "AI-nya gak punya empati." |

**Pattern:** Ganti vague verbs → action konkret + twist + angka spesifik.

## 8 Viral Criteria

Hit minimum **2 criteria per slide**:

| # | Criteria | Description |
|---|----------|-------------|
| 1 | **Pro & Con** | Debate, dua sisi pro/kontra |
| 2 | **Relatable** | Connect ke hidup/kerjaan: gaji, burnout, karir |
| 3 | **Famous figure** | Big name = scroll stopper (Elon, Sam Altman, Google) |
| 4 | **Viral / trending** | Already buzzing, add new angle |
| 5 | **Comedy / irony** | Lucu, absurd twist |
| 6 | **Surprising fact** | Angka/fakta yang bikin geleng |
| 7 | **Emotional hook** | Anger, sympathy, nostalgia, frustration |
| 8 | **Absurd detail** | Detail paling aneh/unexpected |

## Slide Structure

| Slide | Role | Max Words | Key Rule |
|-------|------|-----------|----------|
| **S1** | Hook | 30 kata | MUST follow winning formula |
| **S2** | What | 40 kata | Concrete facts + why it matters |
| **S3** | Tension | 40 kata | Conflict / competing stakes |
| **S4** | Human | 40 kata | One named person + quote/feeling |
| **S5** | Take | 40 kata | Your read on it + implikasi |
| **S6** | CTA | 40 kata | Debate question + callback to S1 |

## Content Rules

- Indo-casual, tech terms tetap English
- Short sentences, one idea each
- Use "baru aja" for immediacy
- Include timing details ("kemarin", "minggu lalu", "bulan ini")
- Find the absurd/surprising angle
- Zero emoji di slides (caption only)
- No em-dash, no AI throat-clearing
- Every sentence passes "text message test"
